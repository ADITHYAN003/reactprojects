Mahendra Singh Dhoni (born 7 July 1981) is an Indian professional cricketer. He was captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from to 2017 and in Test cricket from to 2014. He worked as a Travelling Ticket Examiner at Kharagpur railway station under South Eastern Railway in Midnapore, in West Bengal.
